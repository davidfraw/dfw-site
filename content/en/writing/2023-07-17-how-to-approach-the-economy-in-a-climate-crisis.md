The European Parliament continues to host a passionate debate on what strategic approach we should take in response to the crisis. The combination of the climate and COVID crises in particular has fuelled a debate in which, from my perspective, three main currents have emerged: degrowth, green growth, and "business as usual." Originally, I just wanted to write a short informational summary, but it accidentally turned into a project in which I read the foundational works of each school of thought, spoke off the record with their ideologues and those who actually push for them in practice, and saw more funny graphs than I ever wanted to in my life.
