Hi,

This year marks 20 years since I started making games, mainly larps. It makes sense to me — it brings people joy, and the process of starting with an idea and ending with an actual event, followed by many improving reruns, feels like a creative, meaningful thing.

Larps are also a wonderfully horizontal space which, unfortunately, isn't accessible to absolutely everyone, but the openness and equality is truly great and brings friends from different walks of life.

I have some visions for games I want to make. You can't do everything at once, and I have plenty of commitments on top of that, but there's room for some slow progress and creation.

At the same time, I always welcome more people to help run existing projects, because not only will you bring people joy, but you might also bring something new that improves things. And it's quite a good experience!
