In the summer of 2020, the beloved Czech pastime of "who's abusing welfare the most" turned against a completely absurd target: single mothers. So I did the work, gathered some data, and in an article for Heroine tried to show why the accusations against this vulnerable group are simply disgusting. Unfortunately, the habit of blaming mysterious welfare abusers instead of implementing real reforms of public funds — and blaming one's own incompetence on people in need — has persisted in Czechia to this day.
