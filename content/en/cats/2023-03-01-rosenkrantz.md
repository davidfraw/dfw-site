Together with Guildenstern, he's the oldest — and by far the wisest. He loves listening to the human voice so much that he comes to listen to audiobooks. He can open doors, is always the first to investigate new things, protects the pack, and cuddles wisely and persistently. This is key — Rosenkrantz is always the cuddler, never the cuddlee.
