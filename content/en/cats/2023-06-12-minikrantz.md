Minnie is incredibly active, curious (her main scientific interest is the physics of liquid bodies — she can already turn on the tap by herself) and an absolute terrorist. Besides the other cats, she has an ultra-loyal friend in a stuffed mouse that she carries around and sleeps with. Her activity has reached such intensity that since her arrival, she has consistently won the "Naughtiest Kitty of the Week" award (occasionally sharing it with Titania or Ophelia).
