A short overview of games I've made. I also have some games still in the drawer: you can find them [here](https://drive.google.com/drive/folders/1vHyKBRz7cUy9yF2kZnCP_Uhpzd5kwauL?usp=sharing).

This is only a selection — for instance, [larpovadatabaze.cz](https://www.larpovadatabaze.cz) would show significantly more games.

## Legion: A Siberian Story (2015–present)

A larp about Czechoslovak legionaries in Siberia during WWI. 54 characters, 39 runs. Reportedly the "most famous Czech larp" according to [Games.cz](https://games.tiscali.cz/tema/prozil-jsem-nejslavnejsi-cesky-larp-legie-byl-to-emotivni-mokry-boj-o-zivot-552794).

- Website: [legie.rolling.cz](http://legie.rolling.cz/)
- [Nova TV report](https://tn.nova.cz/zpravodajstvi/clanek/345623-vikend-zazijte-pocit-legionare-pri-dobrodruzne-hre-larp)

## Legion: Siberian Journey (board game)

- Website: [legiongame.cz](http://www.legiongame.cz/)

## The End of History? (~2012–present)

A simulation of international crises inspired by real geopolitics. Players represent factions in different states. 9-10 runs.

- [iForum Charles University — interview](https://iforum.cuni.cz/IFORUMENG-752.html)
- [GitHub: open-source game code](https://github.com/rolling-cz/konec-dejin)

## De la Bête (~2014)

A high-production larp inspired by French novels and legends of the late Ancien Régime. 95 players, 48 hours of play, 12 hours of workshops. Likely the most expensive game in the history of Czech larp at the time.

- [Nordic Larp Yearbook 2014 — article](https://nordiclarp.org/2015/02/06/de-la-bete-an-expensive-beast/)
- Website: part of [rolling.cz](https://rolling.cz/en/category/projects/)

## Čára / The Line (~2019)

A larp about crossing the state border in 1950, created in collaboration with the Institute for the Study of Totalitarian Regimes (ÚSTR).

- [Deník N report from 2019](https://denikn.cz/164833/hodiny-skryvani-napeti-a-strelba-v-lese-vyzkousel-jsem-prechod-statni-hranice-jako-v-roce-1950/)

## Zpěvy rytířské / Chivalric Songs

A larp connecting the essence of knightly history with modern-day props.

- [Games.cz article](https://games.tiscali.cz/tema/stredoveci-rytiri-a-pistole-vynikajici-larp-zpevy-rytirske-ukazuje-ze-to-jde-dohromady-543742)

## Requiem: Reichskinder

A horror larp exploring guilt, punishment, and redemption for Czech Germans after WWII. Set in a fictional re-education institute.

- [Radio Prague International — report](https://english.radio.cz/larp-life-inside-czech-subculture-fantasy-and-foam-swords-8855825)
- [Rascal — interview](https://www.rascal.news/a-czech-larp-group-wrestles-with-nazi-history-by-roleplaying-in-its-bones/)

## Purges (~2022–present)

A darkly comedic larp about political purges, inspired by many totalitarian regimes and modern politics. ~40 players, Czech and international versions.

- Website: [purges.rolling.cz](https://purges.rolling.cz/)

## Bitva pod Horou / Battle Under the Mountain

A large fantasy battle event inspired by The Hobbit.

- Website: [bitvapodhorou.rolling.cz](https://bitvapodhorou.rolling.cz/)

## Fin de Siècle

An intimate 1890s Parisian drama for 28 players. Players become characters at the Le Chat Noir cabaret, two evenings of absinthe-tinged secrets, nostalgia, and turn-of-the-century dilemmas.

## Cave Canem

One of Rolling's longest larps — Wednesday to Sunday.

## 97 Poets of Revachol (~2024–present)

A high-production international larp inspired by the world of Disco Elysium. 109 players, played in the military hospital in Terezín. Two runs in August 2025.

- Website: [poets.rolling.cz](https://revachol.rolling.cz/)
- [Rascal — interview about Rolling and 97 Poets](https://www.rascal.news/a-czech-larp-group-wrestles-with-nazi-history-by-roleplaying-in-its-bones/)
