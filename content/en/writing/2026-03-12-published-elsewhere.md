Besides this blog, I write for other outlets and platforms. Here's an overview:

## Voxpot

I write mainly for [Voxpot](https://www.voxpot.cz/autor/david-wagner/) — a Czech outlet connecting Czechia with the world. Topics: sanctions, geopolitics, international economics.

## Heroine

At [Heroine.cz](https://www.heroine.cz/clanky/autor/70000039-david-frantisek-wagner) I wrote about culture, the internet, and society. Articles include pieces on video games and history, digital regulation, H.P. Lovecraft and ethical consumption, social media, film, and pop culture.

## Larpy.cz

At [Larpy.cz](https://www.larpy.cz/author/daysir/) I write about larp theory, practice, and game reviews. Articles go back to 2008.

## Nordic Larp

I've contributed to [Nordic Larp](https://nordiclarp.org/author/david-frantisek-wagner/) — the international platform for Nordic-style larp. My article on De la Bête was also published in the Nordic Larp Yearbook 2014.

## Talks and Interviews

- [iForum (Charles University)](https://iforum.cuni.cz/IFORUMENG-752.html) — interview about the world of larps
