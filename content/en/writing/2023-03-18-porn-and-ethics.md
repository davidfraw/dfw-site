In the winter of 2020, pornography made front-page news following an extensive report by Nick Kristof that exposed a range of problems and violence even at the largest platforms. In the wake of the strong reaction to these findings, some services completely lost access to customer payments, and there was a lot of talk about how the sex industry actually works.

For Heroine, I described the case including some of its pitfalls. For instance, the strong moral stance of Mastercard and Visa meant that many people dependent on income from these platforms suddenly had no money. I also tried to sketch out what other paths exist toward a more ethical industry.
