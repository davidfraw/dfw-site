I've prepared more than 50 larps, run most of them multiple times ([Legie](http://legie.rolling.cz/) is heading towards its 34th run) and led the development of the vast majority. I'd love to make another fifty. A pretty good introduction to the kind of larps I make is still [this five-year-old TV report](https://tn.nova.cz/zpravodajstvi/clanek/345623-vikend-zazijte-pocit-legionare-pri-dobrodruzne-hre-larp).

I think an interesting perspective is also offered by Čára (The Line), prepared together with the Institute for the Study of Totalitarian Regimes (here's a [2019 report from Deník N](https://denikn.cz/164833/hodiny-skryvani-napeti-a-strelba-v-lese-vyzkousel-jsem-prechod-statni-hranice-jako-v-roce-1950/?cst=c98e83e5398acade3b047f92bbbfdd6e7ab95ad871776e93edbf0cfa06f4ab5f)). Sometimes I venture completely outside historical themes — Arthurian myth, the eternal relationship and value dilemmas, or for instance contemporary Šumava (played in the Highlands), which you can read more about on [Games.cz](https://games.tiscali.cz/tema/stredoveci-rytiri-a-pistole-vynikajici-larp-zpevy-rytirske-ukazuje-ze-to-jde-dohromady-543742).

And well, [here](https://games.tiscali.cz/tema/prozil-jsem-nejslavnejsi-cesky-larp-legie-byl-to-emotivni-mokry-boj-o-zivot-552794) they say Legie is the most famous Czech larp, so I don't even know what to think about that.

I've also made [some educational games with Post Bellum](https://www.postbellum.cz/co-delame/workshopy/tak-tohle-neprojde/) and I'm currently in the final stages of finishing a [board game](http://www.legiongame.cz/). I'd quite like to make another one someday!
