Titania was facing euthanasia because she was born without eyes. So I came back from Brussels a day early and drove with a friend to Opava, where we picked up a tiny blind kitten.
Over time it turned out (after two vet check-ups…) that she was actually a boy. It also turned out that he never grew out of suckling, that he absolutely loves gluten, and that despite being blind, he masterfully hunts, sprawls over people, and causes trouble.
